Our Story

Established in 1998, Bull & Bush Pub & Restaurant began as a humble family-run steakhouse in the heart of Broadhurst. Over the decades, we have grown into an iconic landmark, renowned for our commitment to quality and community.

We specialize in premium Botswana beef, authentic wood-fired pizzas, and a curated selection of beverages. Our philosophy is simple: combine the finest ingredients with genuine hospitality to create unforgettable memories.

"Great food, great company, every single time."

Our Garden & Location

Located in the vibrant suburb of Broadhurst, our expansive garden offers a serene escape from the city. Whether you are joining us for a sundowner, a family lunch, or live music night, our outdoor setting is the perfect backdrop.
